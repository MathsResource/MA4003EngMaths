\mathcal{L} [ k \; \times \; \frac{t}^{n} ] = k \; \times \; \frac{n!}{s^{n+1}}

\mathcal{L} [ 2t^3 ]

$n! = 3 \times 2 \times 1 = 6$

$n+1$ 4


\mathcal{L} [ 2t^3 ] = 2 \times {6 \over s^{3+1}} = $12 \over s^4$
